Sport Specific Training

Whether you are a professional athlete or someone wanting to get ready for your yearly ski vacation, sport specific training is for you. If you want to train to be your best at your sport or are unfortunetly recovering from injury, Shanny has been in both positions and will strive to get you performing better than ever. 

 
 

Ski Conditioing

Alpine and Freeskiing

 

As a former professional alpine racer and slopestyle champion Shanny has been training since she was 12 years old for these disciplines. Her own injuries she has rehabbed include 3 knee surgeries, slipped and herniated discs, clavicle and shoulder injuries. To say she is familiar with training and rehab for this sport is an understatement. 

Motocross Training

Endurance and Strength

 

Motocross is one of the most taxing sports on your body. You need to be extremely strong and have incredible endurance to throw your leg over a bike.
